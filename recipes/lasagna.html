Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is an awesome dish. It consists of dough with a filling of layers of meat and sauce.

Below we will explain how you can cook your own tasty lasagna.

Ingredients

The following ingredients will be required:

  • Lasagna dough.
  • Tomato sauce, or as an alternative a balsamic sauce.
  • Minced meat, any good quality minced meat will suffice.
  • Cheeeese .
  • Some green stuff to sprinkle on the top.

Steps

Follow these steps for cooking your lasagna.

  1. Prepare the ingredients.
  2. Mix the ingredients appropriately.
  3. Cook the ingredients correctly.
  4. Eat the lasagna. You don't have to share, it's okay.
