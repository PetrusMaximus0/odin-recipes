Cheeseburger recipe

A simple meat sandwish augmented with cheese, what is there not to like?

Ingredients

  • Two bread slices
  • 1 Hamburger
  • 1 cheese slice

Steps

  1. Fry the burger
  2. Place burger between bread slices
  3. Place cheese slice between the top bread slice and the hamburger
  4. EAT the cheeseburger. I mean, you could share, if you wanted to, but you don't have to.
