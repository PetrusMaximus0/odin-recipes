Mac and Cheese recipe

Ingredients

Mac and cheese is simple asf and it's quick. The best option for people like me who can't be bothered to cook for too long!

  • Pasta for the mac part.
  • Cheese for the cheese part.
  • Bacon to make it non vegan.
  • Some green stuff to sprinkle on top.

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Cook the pasta for 5 minutes while also frying the bacon.
  3. Add the cheese while the pasta is still hot.
  4. Mix the bacon with the pasta and cheese.
  5. Sprinkle the green stuff because why not.
  6. EAT the pasta , and before you ask, no, you don't have to share.
